Thanks for registering with HelpMyStreet to help people in need. Volunteers like you are making a real difference in your communities by helping people stay safe. Everyone at HelpMyStreet is grateful to you for taking care of them.

To start accepting requests, you'll need to verify your identity.

You can do this now by visiting your Profile Page and clicking the green 'Confirm ID' button.

The process takes just a few minutes. All you need is a passport or driving license and the Yoti app, which you can download for free from your iphone app store or android google play store.

HelpMyStreet.org has chosen to partner with Yoti to verify the identity of our volunteers. Yoti is a trusted digital identification service that provides a fast, secure and free way to verify your identity. You can find out more about ID verification and Yoti in our FAQs.

If there's a problem and you’re unable to verify your identity, let us know by emailing feedback@helpmystreet.org.

Once you're verified, you'll be able to accept help requests from people in your area. To find open requests go to the Open Requests tab in your profile. We’ll also email you when new requests come in near you.

Once you’ve accepted a request, it will appear in your My Accepted Requests tab, so you can keep track of your open requests. From here you can mark a request as complete, or if needed, return it for other volunteers to pick up. If you see a request that can’t be completed, e.g. the contact details don’t work or the request is no longer needed, please let us know by contacting support@helpmystreet.org.

If there’s someone you’re worried about, anywhere in the UK, feel free to submit a Help Request for them.

If you have any feedback about our site, the registration journey, or what you’d like to see next, please email us at feedback@helpmystreet.org.

Thanks again for being part of our community and helping people in need.
